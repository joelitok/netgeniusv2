About

Welcome to Genius University Stablished Since 1898

Genius is a revolutionary online education platform that provides on-demand tutoring and online courses. We believe there are more teachers in the world than are actually teaching so we want to empower more people to learn from each other. Our platform enables thousands of tutors to share their knowledge with students around the globe. Whether you need help with high school algebra or you want to learn how to program in Python, we have a perfect tutor for you. At Genius, we want to change the way you learn.

If you have questions, suggestions or concerns, we'd love to hear from you. Please message us or visit our Help Center.
